About Us

Rooted in faith, driven by excellence, committed to building Kingdom stewardship.

Our Story

The King Makers Consultancy Firm was founded with a vision to merge professional business excellence with biblical principles of stewardship. What began as a passion for empowering entrepreneurs and ministries has grown into a consultancy recognized for integrity, innovation, and impact. Our journey reflects a commitment to raising leaders who not only succeed in business but also honor God in every decision they make.

Over the years, we have walked alongside startups, established enterprises, churches, and organizations, helping them navigate challenges, seize opportunities, and build sustainable models of growth. Every client story is a testimony of faith meeting strategy, and vision meeting execution.

Mission

Our mission is to inspire, equip, and empower entrepreneurs, investors, and leaders to build sustainable businesses and lasting wealth through biblical principles. We believe prosperity is not measured merely by profit, but by stewardship, service, and legacy. Every strategy we design is rooted in values that glorify God and transform generations.

Vision

We envision a future where Kingdom-minded entrepreneurs and investors influence nations through ethical leadership, faithful stewardship, and generational impact. Our goal is to become a leading Christian consultancy recognized globally for raising leaders who embody excellence and integrity while advancing God’s Kingdom in the marketplace.

Kingdom Philosophy

At The King Makers, we believe business is more than transactions — it is ministry, influence, and legacy. Our philosophy is anchored in the conviction that wealth and leadership are entrusted by God to be stewarded faithfully. We teach that true success is measured by impact: transforming communities, uplifting families, and leaving a generational inheritance of faith and excellence.

Leadership

Our leadership team embodies the values of faith, integrity, and innovation. Guided by biblical wisdom and professional expertise, they bring decades of experience in entrepreneurship, investment, and organizational development. The founder represents the heart behind the vision — a leader committed to raising others to walk in purpose and prosperity.

Dr. Eunice Mutava — Founder of The King Makers Consultancy Firm

Whether you are an entrepreneur seeking guidance, a ministry leader pursuing sustainability, or an investor desiring impact, The King Makers Consultancy Firm is here to walk with you. Together, we can build enterprises that honor God, transform communities, and establish generational wealth.
